About Ryan Dixon

Ryan A. Dixon, M.B.A., is a financial executive with 20 years of experience leading finance and operations across high-growth startups and public companies in technology, gaming, retail, and renewable energy sectors.

Ryan has a proven track record in FP&A, M&A, forecasting, compliance, and strategic planning. He is skilled at building high-performing teams, streamlining operations, and driving revenue growth through analytical rigor and strategic leadership.

Throughout his career, Ryan has held executive roles including CFO at DIV Holdings, VP of Finance at Apparent Inc., and Director of FP&A at Wargaming America. He holds an M.B.A. in Strategic Leadership and a B.A. in Economics.

When he’s not managing complex financial operations, Ryan enjoys hiking, woodworking, mentoring students, and paddle boarding.
